    ↓ (40% contacted) → 96 Calls/Consultations
      ↓ (60% interested) → 58 High-interest Leads
        ↓ (30% quote prepared) → 17 Proposals Sent
          ↓ (25% conversion) → 4-5 Deals Closed/month

Average Deal Value: ₹50,000 (parking services)
Monthly Revenue: ₹2,00,000-2,50,000

Without repeat business, break-even on ₹2,50,000 marketing spend
would require: ₹2,50,000 ÷ ₹50,000 = 5 deals/month minimum

With repeat business (existing clients give additional work):
- Break-even: 3-4 deals/month
- Profit margin: 40-50% after service delivery costs
- Annual contract value: Much higher
